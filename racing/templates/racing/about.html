Canadian cross country fans are used to scouring the internet for individual and team race results in PDFs generated by various meet management programs (HY-TEK, Easy Meet Manager, and others).

This site aims to make these results more accessible. That means:

  • Each race has individual and team results
  • Every race is available (limited by the time it takes to upload results)
  • Data is linked together across races (runners, teams, etc.)
  • Results are presented consistently and intuitively in HTML (not PDFs!)

For more information, see the introductory blog post.
